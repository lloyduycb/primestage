[bio]

​Omy Singh is a prominent filmmaker and rapper based in Dubai, renowned for his dynamic contributions to the city's vibrant entertainment scene. His multifaceted career encompasses directing high-profile music videos and delivering compelling rap performances, establishing him as a versatile artist in the industry.​

As a filmmaker, Omy has collaborated with notable artists such as Deep Kalsi, KR$NA, and Kanika Kapoor, showcasing his adeptness at creating visually captivating narratives that complement their musical works. His portfolio includes projects like "Paisa On My Mind" by Deep Kalsi featuring KR$NA, and "80's Ke Songs" by Karma and Deep Kalsi, reflecting his commitment to producing high-quality visual content.​

In addition to his filmmaking endeavors, Omy Singh has made significant strides as a rapper, infusing his music with authentic storytelling and rhythmic prowess. His dual talents in both filmmaking and rapping have solidified his reputation as a creative force in Dubai's entertainment landscape, continually pushing the boundaries of artistic expression.
