DARWIN

Hip-Hop R&B

[bio]

Darwin Rap Artist is continuing the tradition of keeping rap exciting by creating music that is refreshingly new, original and packed with a message. His style of fusion involves blending his admiration of Old School Hip-Hop and R&B with various world music languages and their musical elements.

Darwin is originally from Kerala, born and raised in Dubai, and later moved to London. He was working as an Aerospace Engineer, after attaining his MSc in Aircraft Engineering at Kingston University London. He is also a certified English FA Football Coach, Sports Performance Coach, Leadership Coach as well being Managing Partner and General Manager at a FMCG Company in Dubai.

A man with different hats, Darwin has now partnered with Prime Stage Records and will release music not only for listening pleasure, but also that’s thought provoking and for social change.
