ABOUT PRIMESTAGE RECORDS.

PrimeStage Records is an independent label founded by Jaleel — an artist driven by a love for music, community, and creative freedom. What started as a passion project has become a growing platform for artists to express themselves boldly, without limits. We aim to inspire, connect, and empower through sound.

Diversity is at the core of who we are. From R&B and EDM to hip-hop, indie, and beyond — we embrace every genre and the unique voices behind them. At PrimeStage, we're always exploring, always evolving, and always ready to push the boundaries of what music can be.
